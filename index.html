Pavan Kumar B N

Welcome to my home page. I am glad you have found your way to this site and appreciate your taking the time to visit. I received my Ph.D from Virtual Environmnets Lab, Chung-Ang University, South Korea advised by Professor Young Ho Chai. Previously, I received my Master's degree in Computer Network and Engineering from SIT, India and Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from UBDTCE, India.

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor at CSE Group, Indian Institute of Information Technology, Sri City, India. Prior to this, I worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Naval Postgradute School, Monterey, USA (NRC Research Associate) and as Postdoctoral Fellow at Sejong University, Seoul, South Korea. My research focuses on the field of Human-Robot Interaction, Unmanned Aerial Vehicle, Virtual Reality, Eye Tracking, Human-Computer Interaction, Sensor Fusion and Robotics.
