I completed Ph.D from Virtual Environmnets Lab, Chung-Ang University, South Korea under the supervision of Professor Young Ho Chai. My research focuses on the field of Human-Robot Interaction, Virtual Reality, Eye Tracking, Human-Computer Interaction, Sensor Fusion and Robotics.

Previously, I received my Master's degree in Computer Network and Engineering from SIT, India and Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from UBDTCE, India.
